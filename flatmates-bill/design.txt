Title: Flatmates Bill
Description: An application that gets as input the amount of a bill for a particular period and the days that each of the flatmates stayed in the house for that period and returns how much each flatmate has to pay. It also generates a PDF report stating the names of the flatmate, the period and how much each flatmate had to pay.

Objects: Bill:
           - amount
           - period
         Flatmate:
           - name
           - days_in_house
           - pays(bill)
         PDFReport:
           - filename
           - generate(flatmate1, flatmate2, bill)
